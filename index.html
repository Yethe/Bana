The Bana Fam Tree

The Bana Fam Tree is the genealogical chart or ancestral tree of a particular family of Banana plants. Scroll and explore to see are latest members!

Currently, we have new born Banana plants, which are distributing over the waiting list. If you want to care of a hipster plant with family tree from before they were cool, please fill in the form to adopt a Bana!

If your Bana is already part of the family, you can update your Bana add a new born, or put Bana up for adoption.
